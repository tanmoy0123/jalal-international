OUR STORY

Bridging Talent with Opportunity

Since 1998, Jalal Overseas International has been the trusted bridge between skilled Bangladeshi professionals and leading employers in the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia — building careers and businesses, one placement at a time.

Jalal Overseas International is a leading international recruiting agency specializing in connecting Bangladeshi manpower with employers in the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia.

With decades of experience, we have successfully placed thousands of skilled professionals across construction, healthcare, hospitality, engineering, and more. Our team of experienced recruiters works closely with both clients and candidates to ensure a seamless, transparent, and successful recruitment journey.

Our rigorous screening process — combining extensive interviews, background checks, and skills verification — ensures only the most qualified candidates reach your organization. We don't just fill positions; we build long-term workforce partnerships that drive real business results.

We are a proud member of the Bangladesh Association of International Recruiting Agencies (BAIRA), the apex trade body for recruiting agencies in Bangladesh, giving our clients and candidates access to a verified, regulated, and internationally trusted recruitment framework.
